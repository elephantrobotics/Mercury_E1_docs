# Q&A

This chapter lists common questions about using MiniRobot to control robotic arms for your reference.

**Q1: Are the end effector buttons on the robotic arm only available in the MiniRobot's FreeMove page or under JogMove?**

A: Yes, the end effector button on the robotic arm can only be used in the MiniRobot's FreeMove page or under JogMove, and can also be controlled through Python's Set_Free_Mode interface.
